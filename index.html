About Jacob

My name is Tri Truong, more commonly known as Jacob Truong. I am undertaking my Bachelor of Information Technology degree at RMIT Vietnam, SGS Campus.

In this website, I would like to demonstrate my understanding of the world of Information Technology as well as my original project idea.

Linh Bui

One of the first new friends I made at the university, Linh is a reliable person, with great personality and charisma. Having previously attended Maguire University in Sydney, he has a strong foundation and a considerable sum of experience when it comes to academic work. While somewhat lacking in technical abilities, Linh makes up for it through a great enthusiasm for the field and a fantastic linguistic capability. Furthermore, he scored an impressive 88 on his first assignment of COSC2083, demonstrating  his work ethics and dedication to the craft. Pleasant and diligent, I believe Linh will be the heart of the team, bringing us all together.

Dieu Lam

A quirky and passionate tech enthusiast, Dieu is an asset to any team. He is witty, comical, and very friendly. Being a self-proclaimed technophile, Dieu has great knowledge in both hardware and software. He pursues a dream career in [software engineering], and his determination to reach his objective can be seen through his academic performance. He scored an impressive 96 on his first assignment of ISYS3414 and a 76 for the first assignment of COSC2083, indicating himself to be a high achiever. Bound to be a fierce competitor in the quaternary sector, Lam is a team member that I would prefer to collaborate with more in the future

Anh Nguyen

Delightful, determined and the sole girl among us, Anh brings a peculiar aspect to the dynamic of the group. Seeking to defy social norms and stereotypes, she is insistent on making a name for herself in the male-dominant field of Information Technology. Working towards that goal, Anh dedicates herself to perfecting her skillset, both in the linguistics and technical, and it shows in her academic results. She scored an admirable 96 and 88 on her first assignment of ISYS3414 and COSC2083, respectively, proving herself to be a hardworking and dedicated student. Peculiar and strong-willed, Anh will undoubtedly bring fascinating ideas to the table.
